# Logging In

Access your AirQo Analytics account with your email and password or through a linked social account.

---

## Log In with Email and Password

1. Go to the [AirQo Analytics login page](https://analytics.airqo.net/user/login).
2. Enter your **email address** and select **Continue**.
3. Enter your **password** and select **Login**.
4. You are taken to your [Home page](https://analytics.airqo.net/user/home).

---

## Log In with a Social Account

You can use an existing Google, GitHub, LinkedIn, or X (Twitter) account to sign in or sign up.

1. On the [login](https://analytics.airqo.net/user/login) or [registration](https://analytics.airqo.net/user/creation/individual/register) page, select the icon for the provider you want to use.
2. Complete the provider's sign-in steps.
3. You are returned to AirQo Analytics.
   - If you are new, an account is created automatically.
   - If you already have an AirQo account linked to that provider, you are signed in.

| Provider | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| **Google** | You are asked to choose an account each time. |
| **GitHub** | Uses your GitHub account email. |
| **LinkedIn** | Uses your LinkedIn profile email. |
| **X (Twitter)** | Uses your X profile email. |

:::tip Last used provider
The provider you used most recently is highlighted with a **Last used** badge, so you can sign in faster next time.
:::

:::note Add a password later
If you created your account with a social provider, you can add a password later in **Profile > Security**. This lets you log in with your email and password as well.
:::

---

## Troubleshooting Login

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| "Invalid Credentials" or "Incorrect username or password" | Check that your email and password are correct. Passwords are case-sensitive. |
| Forgot your password | Use the [Reset your password](./resetting-your-password.md) guide. |
| Social login fails | Make sure you select the same provider and account you used to sign up. |
| "Access Denied" | You may not have permission to access this service. Contact your organization administrator or AirQo support. |
