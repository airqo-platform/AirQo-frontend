# Home Dashboard

The Home Dashboard is the first screen you see after logging in. It gives you an at-a-glance view of air quality across your favorite locations and quick shortcuts to the most common actions.

---

## Accessing the Dashboard

- **Individual flow:** Navigate to **Home** in the sidebar.
- **Organization flow:** Navigate to **Dashboard** in the sidebar.

The dashboard loads automatically after you log in.

---

## Dashboard Components

### Favorite Location Cards

Up to four cards display your saved monitoring sites. Each card shows:

- **Real-time PM₂.₅ reading** — the most recent measurement from the site
- **Air quality category** — color-coded based on the reading (Good, Moderate, Unhealthy for Sensitive Groups, Unhealthy, Very Unhealthy, or Hazardous)
- **Site name and location** — city or region where the monitor is installed
- **Trend indicator** — how the current reading compares to recent values

:::tip
Favorite locations are shared between the Home Dashboard and the Map. Add or remove them from either page.
:::

### Trend Charts

The dashboard compares your favorite locations over time:

- **Multi-location time series** — see how PM₂.₅ or PM₁₀ trends compare across your selected sites
- **Adjustable time window** — focus on the last few hours, days, or weeks depending on what is available
- **Click any chart** for a deeper analysis or to export the underlying data

### Quick Actions

Shortcut buttons help you move quickly to common tasks:

- **Explore Map** — open the full interactive map
- **Export Data** — jump to the data export page
- **Add Favorite** — search for and save a new monitoring location

## Reading Air Quality Levels

AirQo uses standard air quality index coloring to make readings easy to interpret:

| AQI Level | Color | PM₂.₅ Range (µg/m³) | Meaning |
|-----------|-------|---------------------|---------|
| **Good** | Green | 0–12 | Air quality is satisfactory |
| **Moderate** | Yellow | 12.1–35.4 | Acceptable for most people |
| **Unhealthy for Sensitive Groups** | Orange | 35.5–55.4 | Sensitive individuals should limit outdoor activity |
| **Unhealthy** | Red | 55.5–150.4 | Everyone may experience health effects |
| **Very Unhealthy** | Purple | 150.5–250.4 | Health alert — avoid prolonged outdoor exertion |
| **Hazardous** | Dark Red | 250.5+ | Emergency conditions |

## Adding a Favorite Location

1. On the Home Dashboard, click **Add Location** or **Customize**.
2. Search for a city or monitoring site.
3. Select up to four locations to display on your dashboard.

For detailed instructions, see [Managing Favorite Locations](../managing-locations/favorite-locations.md).

---

## Organization Dashboard

In the Organization flow, the Dashboard provides a team-level overview:

- Summary of monitoring sites and devices belonging to the organization
- Recent data activity and status
- Quick access to export and visualization tools scoped to the organization's locations

---

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Dashboard shows "No Data" | Your favorite locations may not have recent readings. Try selecting different sites. |
| Favorite cards are missing | You have not saved any favorites yet. Click **Add Location** to get started. |
| Chart values look unexpected | Check that the correct pollutant (PM₂.₅ or PM₁₀) is selected. |
