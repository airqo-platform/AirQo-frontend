# Deleting Your Account

You can permanently delete your AirQo Nexus account from your profile settings.

---

## Before You Delete

Deleting your account:

- Removes your account and personal data from AirQo Nexus
- Revokes your access to all organizations
- Cannot be undone

If you only want to leave an organization, see [Organization Settings](../organization-management/organization-settings.md).

---

## Delete Your Account

1. Select your **Profile** icon and go to **Profile**.
2. Find the **Delete Account** section.
3. Select **Delete Account**.
4. Confirm in the dialog.
5. A confirmation email is sent to your address. Select the link in the email to complete the deletion.

:::danger
Account deletion is permanent. You will need to create a new account and be re-invited to organizations if you want to use the platform again.
:::
