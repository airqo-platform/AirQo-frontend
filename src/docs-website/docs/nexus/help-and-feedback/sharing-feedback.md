# Sharing Feedback

Your feedback helps us improve AirQo Nexus. You can share suggestions, report problems, or tell us what is working well directly from the platform.

---

## Open the Feedback Form

1. Look for the **Share feedback** button at the bottom of the left sidebar.
2. Select it to open the feedback dialog.

:::note
The feedback button is available on most pages. It is hidden only on the system feedback management page used by administrators.
:::

---

## Submit Feedback

1. Choose a **Category**:
   - **General** — general comments or questions
   - **Bug** — something is not working as expected
   - **Feature request** — an idea for a new feature or improvement
   - **Performance** — the platform is slow or unresponsive
   - **UX / Design** — feedback about the look, feel, or ease of use
   - **Other** — anything that does not fit the categories above
2. Describe your feedback in the **Description** field. Include what happened, what you expected, and any steps to reproduce the issue.
3. Optionally, attach a **screenshot**:
   - Select **Capture screenshot** to take a screenshot of your current browser tab. After capturing, you can draw rectangles on the image to highlight specific areas.
   - Select **Upload image instead** to attach an existing image from your device.
   - Supported formats: PNG, JPG, WebP, or AVIF. Maximum file size: 5 MB.
   - If you attach a screenshot by mistake, select **Remove** to delete it.
4. Rate your experience using the star rating.
5. Select **Send feedback**.

:::tip What makes feedback useful?
The more detail you provide, the easier it is for us to act on your feedback. Include the page you were on, the actions you took, and any error messages you saw.
:::

---

## What Happens Next

After you submit feedback:

- Your message is sent to the AirQo team along with your email, the page you were on, your browser version, screen resolution, and any screenshot you attached.
- Sensitive information such as access codes or sign-in details is removed from the page web address automatically.
- You will see a confirmation message when the feedback is sent successfully.
- The team may follow up by email if more information is needed.

---

## Troubleshooting Feedback Submission

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Screenshot capture fails | Make sure your browser supports screen capture and that you grant permission when prompted. |
| Image upload fails | Check that the file is a supported image format and is smaller than 5 MB. |
| Feedback button is missing | You may be on a page where the button is hidden. Navigate to another page and try again. |
