# Deploy a Device to a Site

After Adding a device, you must deploy it to a physical location (a Site) for it to begin tracking meaningful environmental data.

## Prerequisites

* A device that has already been successfully Added or imported into your workspace.

## Steps to Deploy

1. In the left sidebar under Organization Assets, click **Devices**.
2. Locate and select the device you wish to deploy from the list.
3. On the Device Details page, click the **Deploy Device** action button.
4. You will be prompted to select a location. Choose an existing **Site** from the list, or create a new Site by entering the specific latitude and longitude coordinates.
5. Confirm the deployment details. 

Once deployed, the device's status will change to **Deployed**, and its location will be recorded on the AirQo map.
