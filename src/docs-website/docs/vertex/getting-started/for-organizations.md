# For Organizations

Organization Workspaces are designed for scaling and collaboration. To manage a fleet of devices collaboratively, you need to operate within an Organization Workspace.

* **Purpose:** Ideal for NGOs, governments, and research teams deploying a fleet of monitors.
* **Ownership:** Devices belong to the organization entity, not an individual.
* **Features:** Invite multiple team members, assign specific roles, and group devices into Cohorts.

> [!TIP]
> If you are setting up devices for a company or project, always ensure you are switched to your Organization Workspace before Adding devices.

## Getting Started with Your Organization

1. **Switch Workspaces:** Ensure you have selected your Organization from the workspace dropdown in the top right corner. *(Note: If your organization does not exist yet, you must initialize it through the AirQo Analytics platform).*
2. **Invite Your Team:** Navigate to your **Workspace Settings** (often found under your profile icon or a dedicated settings menu). Find the **Team** or **Members** section and click **Invite Member**.
3. **Assign Roles:** Enter the email address of the person you want to invite and select their role:
   * **Admin:** Full access to Add, recall, and manage users.
   * **Editor:** Can manage devices and deployments but cannot alter workspace settings.
   * **Viewer:** Read-only access to device lists and statuses.
4. **Send Invite:** Click **Send Invite** to add them to your team.
