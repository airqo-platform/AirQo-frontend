# For Individuals

Every user has a Personal Workspace attached to their login profile.

* **Purpose:** Ideal for individual users testing a single device at home.
* **Ownership:** You are the sole owner of any devices Added here.
* **Limitations:** You cannot invite other team members to manage devices in a Personal Workspace.

This space allows you to familiarize yourself with the platform and manage your own devices before collaborating with a larger team.

## Exploring Your Workspace

1. **Verify Your Context:** Check the dropdown in the top right corner of the dashboard. It should display your name or a default workspace label.
2. **Review the Empty State:** If you have no devices, your Home dashboard will prompt you to begin adding them.
