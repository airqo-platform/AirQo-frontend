# Supported Manufacturers

Vertex isn't limited to AirQo hardware. You can bring in devices from any onboarded third-party sensor manufacturer (such as AirGradient) and manage them alongside your AirQo fleet. In Vertex, each manufacturer is represented as a **Sensor Manufacturer**.

## What You Can Do with a Third-Party Device

* Manage your entire fleet — AirQo and third-party — from a single dashboard.
* Deploy an imported device to a physical Site, the same way you deploy an AirQo device.
* Control public visibility for imported devices the same way, per Cohort.

## Checking Which Manufacturers Are Supported

There isn't a fixed list of supported manufacturers — Vertex is onboarding new ones over time. To see which are currently available, open the **Sensor Manufacturer** dropdown when you [import a device](./import-devices.md) or [create a Cohort](../device-deployment/device-cohorts.md); it's populated live with every manufacturer currently onboarded.

## Request a New Manufacturer

If your manufacturer isn't in the dropdown, you can request it be added.

1. While importing a device (Single or Bulk), on the **Sensor Manufacturer** field, click **Can't find your Sensor Manufacturer?**
2. In the **Request New Sensor Manufacturer** dialog, your **Full Name** and **Contact Email** are pre-filled from your account.
3. Enter the manufacturer's **Name** and **Official Email** (required), and optionally its **Website**, **Acronym**, and a **Description** of why it should be added.
4. Complete the CAPTCHA if prompted, then click **Submit Request**.

> [!NOTE]
> Your request is reviewed by AirQo before the manufacturer becomes available — it won't appear in the dropdown immediately.
