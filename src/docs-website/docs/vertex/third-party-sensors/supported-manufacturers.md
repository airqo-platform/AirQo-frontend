# Supported Manufacturers

Vertex is built from the ground up to be an **open device deployment platform**. 

## Manufacturer Agnostic
We believe that air quality data should be centralized and accessible, regardless of the hardware used to collect it. You are not locked into using AirQo hardware. 

Vertex allows you to import and manage devices from any supported third-party Air Quality Sensor Manufacturer (such as AirGradient). 

## Centralized Management
By importing your external sensors into Vertex, you can:
* Manage your entire fleet (both AirQo and third-party) from a single dashboard.
* Standardize the data output.
* Deploy external devices to specific physical sites on the AirQo Map.
* Manage data visibility and share your third-party sensor data through the Vertex API.
