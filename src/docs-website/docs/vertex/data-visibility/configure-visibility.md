# Configure Data Visibility

Vertex allows you to control whether your deployed devices are visible on the public AirQo Map, contributing to open data initiatives.

## Steps to Toggle Visibility

1. Navigate to the **Home** dashboard.
2. Scroll down to the **Device Visibility** section.
3. Locate the toggle switch.
   * To contribute to open data, switch the toggle to **Public**.
   * To keep your network private, switch the toggle to **Private**.

> [!WARNING]
> Setting your devices to **Public** means their location and environmental readings will be accessible to anyone viewing the public AirQo Map.
