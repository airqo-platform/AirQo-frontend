# Open Data and Privacy

Vertex gives you absolute control over who can see the environmental data your devices collect.

## Public Devices
When you toggle a device's visibility to **Public**, you are actively contributing to the open data movement. 
* **What happens:** The device's location and its air quality readings will become visible to anyone browsing the public AirQo Map.
* **Benefits:** Your data helps researchers, policymakers, and the public understand air quality trends in your region.

## Private Devices
If you toggle a device to **Private**, the data remains strictly confidential.
* **What happens:** The device will not appear on the public AirQo Map.
* **Access:** Only you, and the team members invited to your Organization Workspace, can view the data and deployment status on the Vertex dashboard.
