# Beacon
*Device health monitoring and maintenance management platform for air quality sensor networks*

---

## 1. Product Name
Beacon

## 2. Brief Product Description
Beacon is a dedicated frontend microservice that provides an interactive web interface for monitoring and analyzing the health of AirQo air quality sensors across Africa in real-time. It acts as the central command center for network uptime, performance analytics, and maintenance tracking.

## 3. Main Purpose
Beacon solves the operational challenge of managing a large-scale, distributed network of air quality monitors. It bridges the gap between hardware deployment and data reliability by providing a unified platform where network administrators and maintenance teams can:

- Monitor live device status and receive real-time alerts
- Visualize performance metrics such as MTBF, MTTR, and uptime percentages
- Manage and schedule maintenance activities while assessing their impact
- Analyze co-location testing for sensor calibration

## 4. Key Features or Capabilities
**Real-time Dashboard**
Live device status monitoring and alerting to ensure rapid response to hardware or connectivity failures.

**Performance Analytics**
Comprehensive visualization of network health metrics including Mean Time Between Failures (MTBF), Mean Time To Repair (MTTR), and overall uptime percentages.

**Maintenance Management**
Scheduling and impact assessment interface that enables proactive and reactive maintenance workflows for deployed sensors.

**Calibration Tools**
Co-location testing analysis dashboard to track and ensure the accuracy of sensor readings over time.

**Historical Reports**
Trend analysis and reporting interface for evaluating long-term network performance and operational efficiency.

## 5. Target Users or Beneficiaries
Beacon is built for anyone involved in the operations, maintenance, and data quality assurance of the AirQo sensor network, including:

- **Network Administrators** managing the operational health of the civic sensor infrastructure.
- **Field Maintenance Teams** scheduling and performing device repairs and deployments.
- **Data Scientists & Researchers** tracking sensor calibration and ensuring data quality.
- **Engineering Teams** monitoring system-wide performance and connectivity.
